Now and before...

Somewhat recent Nashville transplant who grew up in Louisiana while having the pleasure of stopping in Japan, Washington, Missouri, North Carolina and Mississippi on my way. After 6 years as an Air Traffic Controller in the USAF Nashville Software School has been another example of testing my personal capabilities to consume, regurgitate, and actually retain and learn large amounts of new and complex information/skills/theories in a very short period of time. I'm looking forward to starting a new career in a field set that has had a constant grasp on me, since a very young age, and continues to line up perfectly with my continuing passions.
